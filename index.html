About Me

I am a Graphic Designer

Hi, My name is Henok Zelalem, and I am a graphic designer who believes that design can change the world. With a degree in computer science and three years of experience in the industry, I’ve had the opportunity to work with clients in a range of industries, including hospitality, education, and entertainment. My specialties include branding, packaging design, social media graphics and user interface design. I believe that design has the power to evoke emotions and create connections, and I’m always looking for new ways to push the limits of what’s possible.
